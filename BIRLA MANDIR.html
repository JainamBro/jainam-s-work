BIRLA MANDIR

    BIRLA MANDIR is a small town located in the Raigard district of Maharashtra, India. It is renowned for its ancient and revered Rameshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Ganesha . The temple is a significant pilgrimage site and holds great religious importance for devotees.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the Salav village, near Revdanda, Raigad district, Maharashtra which is about 20 km from Alibaug and close to Roha.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    A beautiful marble temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, built by the Birla family, known for its grand architecture, serene surroundings, and panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Revdanda Fort & Beach
  • Kihim Beach
  • Alibaug Beach & Fort
  • Korlai Fort & Lighthouse

STAYCATION
