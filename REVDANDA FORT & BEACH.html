REVDANDA

    Revdanda is a coastal village in Raigad district, Maharashtra, famous for its Portuguese-built Revdanda Fort and serene beach, offering a mix of history, culture, and natural charm.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the Revdanda village, Raigad district, Maharashtra which is about 17 km from Alibaug, near Salav and Korlai.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    A Portuguese-built sea fort with historic ruins, scenic views of the Arabian Sea, and a peaceful beach perfect for relaxation.

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Korlai Fort & Beach
  • Birla Mandir, Salav
  • Kashid Beach
  • Alibaug Fort & Beach

STAYCATION

   Stays are in Revdanda, AlibaugandKashid in homestays, guesthouses & small resorts .

   Cost for stay is ₹1,000 – ₹3,000 per night .

   Food authenic are Malvani cuisine, fresh seafood thalis, vegetarian Konkani meals, and simple homely food in local lodges.
