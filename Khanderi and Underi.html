KHANDERI & UNDERI

    Khanderi and Underi are twin sea forts near Alibaug in Raigad district, Maharashtra, known for their historic Maratha naval significance and scenic island setting.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the two small islands off the coast of Alibaug, Raigad district, Maharashtra which is about 5–6 km from Thal village, accessible by boat.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    Twin sea forts built by the Marathas and later controlled by the British; known for their strategic naval importance, old cannons, temples, and scenic ocean views.

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Alibaug Beach & Colaba Fort
  • Varsoli Beach
  • Kihim Beach
  • Kanhoji Angre Samadhi (Alibaug)

STAYCATION
