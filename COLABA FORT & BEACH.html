COLABA

    Colaba Fort, located off the coast of Alibaug in Raigad district, Maharashtra, is a sea fort built by Shivaji Maharaj, famous for its rich history, temples, and stunning sea views.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the coastal town of Alibaug, Raigad district, Maharashtra which is about 32 km from the Alibaug shore.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    A historic sea fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 1680, known for its strategic location, old temples, freshwater wells, and panoramic views of the Arabian Sea .

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Revdanda Fort & Beach
  • Korlai Fort & Beach
  • Murud-Janjira Fort
  • Alibaug town
