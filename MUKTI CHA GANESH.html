MUKTI CHA GANESH

    Mukti cha Ganesh is in a small town located in the Ratnagiri district of Maharashtra, India. It is renowned for its ancient and revered MUKTI CHA GANPATI Temple, dedicated to Lord Ganesh. The temple is a significant pilgrimage site and holds great religious importance for devotees.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the Kelshi village, Dapoli Taluka, Ratnagiri district, Maharashtra which is about 30 km from Dapoli town.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    An ancient Ganesh temple believed to fulfill wishes and grant spiritual liberation (Moksha), attracting devotees especially during Ganesh Chaturthi.

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Kelshi Beach
  • Anjarle Ganpati Temple
  • Harnai Port
  • Dapoli town
