KHASID

    Kashid is a famous beach town in Raigad district, Maharashtra, known for its white sandy shores, crystal waters, and peaceful surroundings, making it a perfect getaway.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the Raigad district, Maharashtra at Alibaug–Murud road which is about 30 km from Alibaug and close to Revdanda and Korlai.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    One of the cleanest and most popular beaches in Konkan, famous for its white sand, crystal-clear water, and adventure activities like water sports and horse riding .

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Revdanda Fort & Beach
  • Korlai Fort & Beach
  • Murud-Janjira Fort
  • Alibaug town

STAYCATION

   Stays are in beachside cottages, homestays, and mid-range resorts near Kashid.

   Cost for stay is ₹1,500 – ₹4,000 per night .

   Food authenic are Malvani fish thali, solkadhi, veg Konkani meals, and beach shacks offering snacks.
