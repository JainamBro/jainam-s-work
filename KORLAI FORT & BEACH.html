KORLAI

    Korlai is a small coastal village in Raigad district, Maharashtra, known for its Portuguese-built Korlai Fort and scenic beach, offering both history and natural beauty to visitors.

WHERE IT IS ?

     It is located in the Revdanda, Raigad district, Maharashtra which is about 20 km from Alibaug, close to the Birla Mandir at Salav.

IMPORTANCE OF THIS PLACE :-

    A Portuguese-built sea fort with historic ruins, scenic views of the Arabian Sea, and a peaceful beach perfect for relaxation.

NEARBY PLACES FOR VISIT :-
  • Birla Mandir, Salav
  • Kashid Beach
  • Alibaug Fort & Beach
  • Revdanda Fort

STAYCATION

   Stays are in Alibaug, Revdanda, andKashid in homestays, guesthouses & small resorts .

   Cost for stay is ₹1,200 – ₹3,500 per night .

   Food authenic are Local Malvani seafood, veg thalis, and Konkani-style homemade meals; plenty of beachside eateries .
